## Future Skills

### /health-review — Case Review
Review a long health "case" — multiple visits, test results, treatment changes over time — and highlight the key patterns, contradictions between providers, and important trends. Think of it as a case summary that connects the dots across a complex health journey.

**Why:** Complex health situations involve many doctors, many visits, and many data points. Patients lose the thread. A skill that synthesizes the full picture and flags "doctor A said X but doctor B said Y" would be genuinely valuable.

**Context:** This requires the user to bring context (paste notes, describe history). Future persistent patient context would make this even more powerful, but the skill works without it — the user just provides the case narrative.
